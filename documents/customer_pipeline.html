Loan Approval Pipeline

Step-by-step journey of a customer applying for a loan

Step 1: Loan Application

The customer fills out a loan application using a web or mobile app. Details include personal information, income, employment, bank account, and required KYC documents.

Step 2: Data Verification

The system validates the submitted details, checks for missing or incorrect information, and verifies identity using KYC services.

Step 3: Credit & Financial Check

Credit score and financial history are fetched from credit bureaus and bank statements to understand the repayment capability.

Step 4: Eligibility Check

Basic eligibility rules such as age, income threshold, and employment criteria are applied. Ineligible applications are rejected instantly.

Step 5: Risk Assessment

A machine learning model evaluates the risk and predicts the probability of default based on the customer's financial profile.

Step 6: Loan Decision

The system automatically approves or rejects the loan. In rare cases, the application may be sent for manual review.

Step 7: Loan Offer

If approved, the customer receives a loan offer with amount, interest rate, tenure, and EMI details. The customer can accept digitally.

Step 8: Disbursement

After acceptance, the loan amount is transferred directly to the customer’s bank account.

Step 9: Repayment & Monitoring

The customer repays EMIs as scheduled. The system continuously monitors payments and sends reminders if required.
